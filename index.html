Chalino Sanchez

The King of the Corrido

Why Chalino was one of the best regional Mexican singers.

Rosalino Sanchez was born on August 30 1960. He was a Mexican song writer before he actually started singing his own corridos. People would pay good money so Chalino could make them lyrics.

Chalino was the youngest out of the 7 children. According to a family friend, Sanchez escaped to Tijuana, Mexico with nothing but his gun and Jesus Malverde. During his time in Tijauana he worked as a pollero, a pollero is someone who helps people cross the border. In 1977 he moves to Los Angeles California. In 1984 he was married to Marisela Vallejos. When they got married their first son, Adan Sanchez was already on the way. They were married until his death in 1992. They shared two children, Adan Sanchez and Cynthia Sanchez. On may 16 1992 he was murdered after a show in Culiacan.
